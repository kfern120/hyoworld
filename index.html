Hyo

A MYO Powered gesture keyboard to communicate, bring simplicity and ease to those with hearing difficulties or hearing impairment. With HYO, communication is as simple as a flick of the wrist!

About Hyo

HYO is a project designed to ease digital communication with the use of gesture control through the MYO Armband. HYO came into existence when our team forged friendships at HackWestern2, a hackathon held in London, Ontario, Canada. Interpretation and communication are essential to our nature, when it is difficult to do so we are limited in our thoughts and actions. We believe that life shouldn't be limited. It should be limitless, and that is why we developed HYO, to create a progressive difference in people's lives.
